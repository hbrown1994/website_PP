cleave

This work proposes a method for collaboratively utilizing sound within an environment or space, aiming to expand the possibilities of sound.

The term "cleave" refers to actions such as "dividing" or "integrating" continuous experiences. Users are invited to engage with phenomena that arise between the grids: predictions, states of resemblance, comparisons with memory, and irregular occurrences.

Through cleave, users gain new perceptions by recognizing, evaluating, and focusing on these phenomena. This process also includes actions like walking, breathing, and other bodily functions.

The shared experience of cleave enables collaborative utilization of acquired perceptions, fostering a deeper connection between individuals and their environment.
